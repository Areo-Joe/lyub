# ⏱️ Lyub - Time Tracker

A simple time tracking app inspired by the Lyubishchev method — track how you spend your time and understand your habits.

## Features

- **Start & Stop Timer** — One tap to start tracking, one tap to stop
- **Categories** — Organize your time into Creative Work, Routine Tasks, Rest, and Personal
- **Custom Categories** — Create your own categories with custom colors
- **Activity History** — See all your past activities grouped by day
- **Statistics** — View daily and weekly breakdowns of how you spend your time
- **Flexible Time Display** — Switch between seconds, minutes, or hours

## How It Works

1. Pick a category (like "Creative Work" or "Rest")
2. Tap Start when you begin
3. Tap Stop when you're done
4. Check your Stats to see where your time goes

## Privacy

All your data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.
